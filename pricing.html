Frequently asked questions

What is the cost of the KA Daily app?

The KA Daily app offers several subscription options to fit your needs:
- Monthly Subscription: $14.99 per month
- Quarterly Subscription: $39.99 every 3 months
- Annual Subscription: $99.99 per year

The app itself is free to download in the iOS, Google Play, Apple TV, Amazon Appstore, or Roku Store.

What features are included with each subscription plan?

All subscription plans include:
- Access to a library of workout routines
- Programmed guided fitness plans
- Access to a library of easy, healthy recipes
- Progress tracking tools
- Community forum
- Exclusive content from Katie Austin

 Can I try the app before subscribing?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial for new users. During the trial period, youll have access to all features of the app. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends to avoid being charged!

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription directly through the My Account page on our site here:
1. Select Subscription.
2. Hit Cancel
3. Follow the prompts to cancel your subscription.

If you originally subscribed via the app store, you can also manage your subscription through your app store account settings. Be sure to cancel before the end of your current billing cycle to avoid being charged for the next period.

What happens if I cancel my subscription before the end of the billing cycle?

If you cancel your subscription, you will still have access to the apps features until the end of your current billing period. Once the period ends, your subscription will not renew, and you will not be charged further.

Can I get a refund if Im not satisfied with the app?

Please note that all purchases are non-refundable. We encourage you to review your order carefully before completing your purchase. Our team is always here to assist you with any questions prior to finalizing your order.

Are there any additional fees or hidden costs?

No, there are no additional fees or hidden costs. The prices listed on our pricing page are the total cost of the subscription. Any in-app purchases will be clearly indicated and are optional.

Can I switch between different subscription plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan at any time. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle, and any applicable adjustments in the subscription fee will be applied.

 Is my subscription automatically renewed?

Yes, subscriptions are automatically renewed at the end of each billing cycle. 

 How can I contact support if I have more questions about pricing?

For any additional questions or concerns about pricing, please reach out to our support team at asst@getfitwithkatie.com.  Were here to help!
