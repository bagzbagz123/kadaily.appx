Open Face Turkey Sammy’s

Open Face Turkey Sammy’s

These Open Face Turkey Sammy's are piled high with turkey, avocado, and fresh veggies, they make for a satisfying meal that's full of flavor!
Print Recipe Pin Recipe Add to Collection Go to Collections
Total Time 15 minutes mins
Course Main Course
Servings 2

Ingredients
  

  • 2 slices toast
  • 1.5 tablespoons chipotle aioli
  • 6 slices deli turkey
  • 1/2 heirloom tomato, sliced
  • 2 slices havarti cheese
  • 4 pieces bacon
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup arugula
  • salt & pepper, to taste

Instructions
 

  • Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  • Spread 3/4 tablespoon of chipotle aioli on each slice of toast.
  • Layer each toast with 2-3 slices of turkey.
  • Top with tomato slices, havarti cheese, cooked bacon, red onion, and avocado slices.
  • Sprinkle arugula on top of each open-faced sandwich.
  • Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
Keyword Bacon, Cheese, Sandwich, Tomato, Turkey
