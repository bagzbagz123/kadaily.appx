Frequently asked questions

How can I find upcoming events?

Stay tuned for upcoming events by:
1. Joining the Katie Austin Mailing List (insert link to sign up form for newsletter)
2. Follow us on Eventbrite

Are there any events that require payment?

Some events may require a ticket fee. Event details will specify if there is a cost involved. For events that are free, registration may still be required to secure your spot.

What if I need to cancel my registration for an event?

If you need to cancel, please email our team at asst@getfitwithkatie.com. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the event.

Are events accessible to everyone?

Yes, events are generally designed to be accessible to all!

Can I suggest an event or topic for future events?

Yes! We welcome suggestions for future events. You can submit your ideas by contacting us through the apps feedback feature or by emailing us at asst@getfitwithkatie.com. 

Who should I contact if I have issues with my ticket for an event?

For any issues related to events, such as technical difficulties or questions about registration, please contact our support team at asst@getfitwithkatie.com. Well assist you with any concerns you may have.
